About Us

There are many different things there are to know about us. For starters, we are based in Northern Ireland, specifically in Derry. We offer many things, such as merchandise from various artists, including your favourites. We of course offer food and drink because it will be a long day of fun and excitement! Our artists, such as Esdeekid, A$AP Rocky etc. come from many different areas around the world, including Liverpool, LA, NYC so on and so on.

This course also spans across a few days, with much more artists and events to come as the party goes on! As well as many other fun activities for you to enjoy, such as karoake, where you get to choose a song of your liking to sing or even a song that will be played in the festival. There will also be fun games to play such as ping pong and badmington to add onto the immense fun you will be having!

We are also sponsored by many local businesses, such as Seagate for helping us make this event become possible in the first place, Eurospar, Pikes & Pommes for the food and drink that will be provided at the event. As well as many other local businesses that support this event, makikng it possible in the first place.
